# Modernization Decision Matrix

The Modernization Decision Matrix maps LCI scores and business drivers to the appropriate modernization strategy.

| LCI Score | Business Criticality | Recommended Strategy |
|----------|----------------------|----------------------|
| Low      | Low                  | Rehost              |
| Low      | High                 | Replatform          |
| Medium   | Medium               | Refactor            |
| High     | High                 | Rearchitect         |
| Critical | Any                  | Replace / Retire    |

This matrix ensures consistent, architecture-led modernization decisions across the lending portfolio.

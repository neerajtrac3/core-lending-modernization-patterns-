# Modernization Strategy Classification

Modernization strategies are classified using two dimensions:

## 1. Legacy Complexity Index (LCI)
- Low (1–10) → Rehost / Replatform  
- Medium (11–18) → Refactor  
- High (19–24) → Rearchitect  
- Critical (25–30) → Replace / Retire  

## 2. Business Criticality
- Regulatory  
- Customer Experience  
- Operational Risk  
- Revenue Impact  

The combination determines the modernization path.
